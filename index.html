About Me

Hello, my name is Abby, and I hail from Nigeria.
I consider myself a kind person, although I admit I'm quite shy.
Despite my reserved nature, those close to me might not believe I'm actually shy when we get to know each other better.
My Favourite colour is Purple. I miss my Dad and my mum so much.
In my life, I am fortunate to have a best friend, Mrs. Oluyomi Ibikunle, with whom I've shared a deep bond for approximately 16 years.

I've made some wonderful friends here Maggi, Uche, Bola, Sylvia, and Bella. They've been exceptionally kind to me.
I have a younger sister and a not so little brother (Enny and Papa D).
I also have a big sister, along with her husband and three children. Interestingly, these three kids love me dearly, and I reciprocate their affection.
We spent the holidays together, celebrating Christmas, New Year, and even my birthday
I fondly remember the days when people thought Shawn was my son. It's remarkable to see how much he has grown; he's now even taller than me.

One of the things that would make me happy would be me achieving my goal of becoming a data analyst at the end of this course.
I'm dedicating all my focus and attention to this pursuit.
Initially, I felt overwhelmed, but taking a breather and having an amazing tutor who supports me has made a world of difference.
With her guidance, I am confident I will excel.
I can't believe i created my very first website. It may not be perfect, but I'm immensely proud of this accomplishment.
I want to express my heartfelt thanks to everyone who believed in me, providing the courage and support I needed. Thank you so much. I love each and every one of you.
